Hi, I’m Shayan Hezarkhani.

I’m a motion designer driven by storytelling and precision. My work spans explainer videos, product promos, music videos, and 2D/3D animation—often weaving in illustration and sound for a fuller, more cinematic feel. With roots in theatre and visual arts, I love turning complex ideas into simple, memorable motion systems and immersive scenes. I care about visuals that are minimal, refined, and built to connect.
